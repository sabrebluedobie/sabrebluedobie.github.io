Meet Sabre

The Office Manager

Sabre's my blue Doberman. He's been with me through every project, every deadline, every debugging session. When I started building websites, he was there. When I almost died from that Shopify project last year, he was there.

He supervises. He judges. He reminds me to take breaks. This whole company is named after him.

How He Got Here

Born in Oklahoma. Smart from day one. My husband brought him home, and Sabre immediately decided I needed supervision.

When I started learning web development, Sabre was my constant. Lying on his rug while I figured out JavaScript. Staring at me when I'd been at the computer too long. Being the calm presence when projects got stressful.

So when I finally launched Bluedobie Developing, there was only one name that made sense.

What He's About
