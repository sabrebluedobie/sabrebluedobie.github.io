Explore a blocky world and build whatever you like!

Desktop: WASD to move, mouse to look, Space to jump. Left click breaks a block, right click places one. Press 1-6 to pick a block, F to toggle Fly mode.

Tablet/Phone: use the joystick to move, drag the right side of the screen to look, and tap Jump / Break / Place.
