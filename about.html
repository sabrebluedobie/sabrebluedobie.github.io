About Bluedobie Developing

I'm Melanie Brown. I build small websites for people who need them done right, without the wait or the agency price tag.

What I Actually Do

I build landing pages, portfolio sites, and simple business websites for solopreneurs and small businesses. Projects under $3k. Fast turnaround. No 6-month timelines.

I'm based in Princeton, Kentucky, serving Western KY and beyond. You work directly with me - no account managers, no committees, no agency overhead.

Most projects take 2-4 weeks from start to launch. You get a functional, mobile-friendly site that actually works.

How Bluedobie Works

You contact me. We talk about what you need. I give you a clear quote. You approve it, I build it, you launch it. Most projects take 2-4 weeks from start to finish.

How I Got Here

My dad taught me DOS as a kid on a computer he built from scratch. We moved around a lot (military family), and technology was the constant. I watched him build things, and I wanted to do the same.

I'm entirely self-taught. No bootcamp, no CS degree - just figuring things out, breaking things, fixing them. I learned from Google, University of Michigan courses, and years of trial and error.

Bluedobie Developing is named after my blue Doberman, Sabre. He's been my office companion through every project.
