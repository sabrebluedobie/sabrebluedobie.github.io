Our Journey



Welcome to the journey of Bluedobie Developing! My name is Melanie Brown, the founder of this innovative web design company in western Kentucky. My love for technology began in my childhood, thanks to my father's dedication and passion for computers. Growing up in a military family, our frequent relocations led us to various places, including rural Southern Missouri and Kansas City, Missouri. It was here that my fascination with technology was ignited as I watched my father build our first computer from scratch.

These formative experiences, coupled with formal training from reputable institutions like Google and the University of Michigan, allowed me to hone my web design skills, leading to the establishment of Bluedobie Developing. Our company, inspired by my beloved blue Doberman, Sabre, blends my childhood influences with my passion for technology to offer custom web design and professional web design services.

  • Creative Web Solutions: From e-commerce websites to portfolio sites, our creative web solutions encompass a wide range of services designed to meet diverse business needs.
