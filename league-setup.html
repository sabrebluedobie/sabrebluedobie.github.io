What You Need to Find:

1Ô∏è‚É£ ESPN League ID

This is the easiest one! Your League ID is in your browser's address bar.

Steps:

  1. Go to your ESPN Fantasy Football league homepage
  2. Look at the URL in your browser's address bar
  3. Find the number after leagueId=

Example URL:

https://fantasy.espn.com/football/league?leagueId=1234567&seasonId=2024

Your League ID would be: 1234567

2Ô∏è‚É£ ESPN s2 Cookie

The s2 cookie is needed to access your private league data.

For Chrome Users:

  1. While on your ESPN Fantasy page, press F12 (or right-click ‚Üí "Inspect")
  2. Click the "Application" tab at the top
  3. In the left sidebar, expand "Cookies"
  4. Click "https://fantasy.espn.com"
  5. Find "espn_s2" in the Name column
  6. Double-click the Value to select it all (it's long!)
  7. Copy it (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C)

For Safari Users:

  1. Go to Safari ‚Üí Preferences ‚Üí Advanced
  2. Check "Show Develop menu in menu bar"
  3. On ESPN page: Develop ‚Üí Show Web Inspector
  4. Click the "Storage" tab
  5. Click "Cookies" ‚Üí "https://fantasy.espn.com"
  6. Find "espn_s2" and copy the value

For Firefox Users:

  1. Press F12 on ESPN page
  2. Click the "Storage" tab
  3. Expand "Cookies"
  4. Click "https://fantasy.espn.com"
  5. Find "espn_s2" and copy the value

What it looks like:

AECAbcd123xyz789...really long string...abc123

Starts with "AE" and is very long (100+ characters)

Surrounded by curly brackets { } with letters, numbers and dashes
