Looking Ahead: Bifrost

A developing idea, not a finished product. Here's what we're exploring next.

What Bifrost Is

Bifrost is a concept we're developing for AI workflow routing and business continuity planning. The idea: help a small business owner figure out what kind of help they actually need, then route them to the right next step — instead of leaving them to sort through a pile of options on their own.

Bifrost is not available yet. It's not for sale, and it's not fully built. This page exists so people can understand where Bluedobie is headed, not to sell something that isn't ready.

What It's Meant to Solve

Reducing Owner Overwhelm

Helping business owners decide what kind of help they need, without having to research it all themselves.

AI Workflow Routing

Pointing a request toward the right tool or process, instead of one-size-fits-all automation.

Business Continuity Planning

Helping a business think through what needs to keep running, and how.

Unexpected Absence Support

Planning for the unplanned — illness, injury, or a death in the family — so the business isn't left stranded.

Keeping Operations Moving

Making sure basic operations can continue when the owner is unavailable, even briefly.

Where We're Starting

Bifrost will likely start as a local-first system, tested with a small group of Western Kentucky businesses before it goes any further. We're not promising a national rollout or a launch date. This is exploratory work, and we'd rather be honest about that than oversell it.
