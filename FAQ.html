Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers!

General Questions

What is Bluedobie Developing?
Bluedobie Developing is a small business web design and content systems company founded by Melanie Brown. We build practical websites and free web audits locally in Western Kentucky, and support small businesses nationally through DobieCore Suite, our brand voice-based content system.
Who is Melanie Brown?
Melanie Brown is the founder of Bluedobie Developing. She's self-taught, based in Princeton, Kentucky, and builds practical digital systems for small businesses that don't have time for bloated tech stacks. Read more on the About page.
Who is Sabre?
Sabre is Melanie's blue Doberman and the reason the company is named Bluedobie. He's part of the brand personality — office manager, project supervisor, and occasional social media star. Meet Sabre.

Website Help

What website services do you offer?
Basic small business websites, light website edits and updates, and website cleanup for pages that are broken, confusing, or slow. See the Services page for details.
Do you only work with businesses in Western Kentucky?
Local web design and website help is based in Western Kentucky. DobieCore Suite, our content system, is available to small businesses nationally.
What's your pricing structure?
Pricing depends on the project. Small business websites and light edits are quoted after a quick conversation about what you need — no surprise invoices.

Free Web Audit

How does the free web audit work?
You request an audit, we review your site for things like clarity, mobile usability, calls to action, local SEO basics, and navigation, then send you a plain-language summary of what to fix first. No pressure, no obligation. See the Free Web Audit page for the full list of what's covered.
Is the audit really free?
Yes. There's no cost and no requirement to book anything afterward.

DobieCore Suite

What is DobieCore Suite?
DobieCore Suite is a brand voice-based content system that helps small businesses with prewriting, captions, images, blogs, and a content calendar template — all built from one consistent brand voice. Explore DobieCore Suite.
How much does DobieCore Suite cost?
Signing up is free, and no credit card is required until you pass the free usage limits.
Is DobieCore Suite only for Western Kentucky businesses?
No. DobieCore Suite is available nationally to any small business that wants brand-consistent content support.

Looking Ahead: Bifrost

What is Bifrost?
Bifrost is a developing concept for AI workflow routing and small business continuity planning — including support for unexpected owner absence. It is not available yet. Read more on the Looking Ahead page.
Can I sign up for Bifrost now?
Not yet. It will likely start as a local-first test with a small group of Western Kentucky businesses. If you're curious, reach out and we'll keep you posted.
